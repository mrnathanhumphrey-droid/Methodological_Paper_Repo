# Stalled-Recovery Configuration — POL Watch Doc

**Purpose:** Track the textbook STALLED-recovery configuration identified by Carnegie 2025 ("captured Constitutional Court that survives parliamentary turnover + cohabitation with old-regime presidency"). POL is the live test case 2025-2027.

**Locked:** 2026-05-25
**Status:** OPEN — predictions locked, awaiting unfolding events

---

## The configuration

Per Carnegie Endowment 2025 ("U.S. Democratic Backsliding in Comparative Perspective"), of 25 backsliding episodes since 1990, only 4 have fully recovered, and only Sri Lanka >5 years sustained — and even SRI reversed in 2019. The textbook STALLED-recovery configuration:

1. **Captured Constitutional Court survives parliamentary turnover**
2. **Captured Court is itself reform-immune** (can strike down restoration legislation while protected from change by tenure rules)
3. **Cohabitation with old-regime-aligned presidency**
4. **Constitutional Court + opposition presidency = restoration legislation blocked**

## POL is the live test (2025-2027)

**Current configuration as of 2026-05-25:**
- **Tusk-led reformist coalition holds the Sejm** (October 2023 election win)
- **PiS-captured Constitutional Tribunal still in place** (judges appointed 2015-2023 with extended terms)
- **President Karol Nawrocki (PiS-backed) won the June 2025 presidential election** — opposition presidency now in place, succeeding the also-PiS-aligned Andrzej Duda
- **Restoration legislation faces double-veto risk**: President can veto, Constitutional Tribunal can strike

**The V-Dem signal:** POL libdem 2022 → 2025 = 0.417 → 0.645 (Δ+0.228). Sub-indicators showed dramatic reversal (high court independence +1.69, media censorship index +2.47). But: V-Dem measures EFFECTIVE institutional function, and the underlying CAPTURE remains in the Constitutional Tribunal + now the presidency.

**Carnegie 2025's prediction**: this configuration produces STALLED recovery — meaning either (a) initial gains reverse as captured institutions block legislation, or (b) gains plateau and the country sits in a hybrid state.

## Reference cases (for comparison)

### Sri Lanka 2015-2019 (the prototype stalled-then-reversed case)
- Sirisena 2015 election produced ~0.10 libdem recovery
- 2019 election: Rajapaksa returned, recovery fully reversed
- **Mechanism**: judiciary remained partly captured, opposition fragmented during reform window

### Brazil 2003-2010 (Lula's previous restoration era — semi-stalled)
- Lula's restoration period had some democratic backsliding under PT later
- Recovery wasn't fully durable

### South Korea 1987 (clean recovery)
- Constitutional amendment opened election competition
- Captured court was REPLACED, not just out-voted
- Recovery durable through 2025 (though current pressure rising)

### Indonesia post-1998 (slow durable recovery)
- Suharto resigned
- Constitutional Court created fresh in 2003
- Recovery durable through 2010s with some erosion after

**Pattern**: durable recoveries require **constitutional court replacement** or **constitutional amendment**, not just operating around captured courts.
