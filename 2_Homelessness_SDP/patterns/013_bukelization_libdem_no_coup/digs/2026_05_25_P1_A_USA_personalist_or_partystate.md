**Status after**: closed-supported as HYBRID (new typology cell)
**Method**: V-Dem party-system indicators 2010-2025 for USA + benchmark cases (HUN/POL party-state vs SLV/TUR personalist)

## Method

V-Dem party-system sub-indicators used:
- **v2psoppaut** — opposition party autonomy
- **v2pscohesv** — party cohesion (high = unified party machine)
- **v2psorgs** — party organizational strength
- **v2psnatpar** — national party organization (branch structure)
- **v2psprbrch** — party branches density
- **v2psprlnks** — party-state programmatic linkages

For each indicator, USA Trump II era values compared to party-state benchmarks (HUN/POL) and personalist benchmarks (SLV/TUR/VEN/BLR).

## Reading the V-Dem indicators

### USA's distinguishing feature
**v2pscohesv (party cohesion) = 0.39 in 2024-2025** — substantially lower than ALL other cases in our corpus. USA party cohesion has been low throughout the Trump era:
- 2010: 0.71
- 2018: 0.48
- 2022: 0.26
- 2024-2025: 0.39

Even before Trump, USA pscohesv was structurally LOW (0.71 in 2010) because of USA's federalist + primary-system + weak-party-discipline tradition. So low cohesion isn't NEW under Trump — it's the baseline.

**This is methodologically important**: low pscohesv in USA doesn't mean Trump is the only thing holding GOP together. GOP cohesion has always been low by V-Dem measure relative to consolidated parties elsewhere.

### Counter-evidence — USA's institutional infrastructure
Despite low V-Dem party cohesion, USA has features that match party-state profile:
- **Federalist Society network** (1982+) — institutional judicial-appointment pipeline; predates Trump
- **SCOTUS 6-3 composition** — institutional, persists past leader; not Trump-personal
- **State-level GOP capture** — Republican Legislative Campaign Committee + state party machinery built since Tea Party era 2010+; election infrastructure capture in GOP-controlled states
- **Heritage Foundation Project 2025** — institutional preparation for executive consolidation; party-machine product, not Trump-personal
- **GOP-aligned media ecosystem** — Fox News, Sinclair, talk radio, online networks — institutional + persists

These features are not captured well by V-Dem's party-cohesion measure (which focuses on legislative voting discipline + party platform alignment) but ARE party-state infrastructure.

### Trump I vs Trump II comparison
Trump I (2017-2020) plateaued; Trump II (2025) is accelerating. The DIFFERENCE is not Trump's personality (same person) but the institutional readiness:
- SCOTUS 6-3 (by 2020)
- State-level election infrastructure (2020-2024)
- Project 2025 (built 2023-2024)
- GOP party purges of dissidents (Liz Cheney etc., 2021-2024)

This suggests the GOP party-state infrastructure was BUILT between 2020 and 2024 — Trump activated it on inauguration. Pure personalist would have produced Trump-I-style plateau again.

## Verdict — HYBRID classification

USA Trump II doesn't fit cleanly into either pure personalist or pure party-state. It is a **HYBRID**: personalist-led + party-state-backed.

| Dimension | Personalist signature | Party-state signature | USA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Leader-brand dominance | YES | not necessarily | **YES** |
| Succession-crisis risk on leader exit | YES | NO | **YES** (Trump 78, term-limited 2029) |
| Independent party identity beyond leader | NO | YES | **PARTIAL** (GOP existed before Trump but is brand-fused now) |
| Pre-existing institutional party infrastructure | NO | YES | **YES** (Federalist Society, state machines, SCOTUS) |
| Party-machine election apparatus | weak | strong | **STRONG** (state-level + RNC) |
| Party-state programmatic linkages (v2psprlnks) | low (0.0-0.8) | mid-high (1.0-2.5) | **2.49 — highest among any backsliding case** |
| Party legislative cohesion (v2pscohesv) | varies | high (1.5-2.7) | **0.39 — LOW (structural USA feature, not Trump-specific)** |

USA shows 2 personalist signatures + 5 party-state signatures = predominantly party-state-backed but personalist-led.

## Refinement of the 2x2 typology

The original 2x2 (speed × institutional vehicle):
- single-event × personalist: SLV, TUN, USA, NIC
- single-event × party-state: VEN late-stage (rare)
- incremental × personalist: TUR, BLR, SRB
- incremental × party-state: HUN, POL, IND, BGD

USA reassignment: **single-event × HYBRID (personalist-activated party-state)**. This is a 5th cell.

**Refined predictions for HYBRID:**
- Consolidation speed: FAST (single-event-driven, matches Trump II 2025 fast-pole)
- Durability post-leader-exit: MODERATE to HIGH (party-state infrastructure persists; not full personalist collapse)
- Recovery prospects: STALLED (per PRE_REG_006 — captured SCOTUS + GOP state apparatus = stalled-recovery configuration)

This refines PATTERN_026 (USA fast-pole): the speed is personalist-style, but the durability is party-state-style. Worst of both worlds for recovery prospects.
