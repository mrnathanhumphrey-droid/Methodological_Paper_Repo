## Critical refinement — federal-friction is BREAKABLE

The single most important finding from this analysis: **federal-friction is a TEMPORARY slow-burn force, not a permanent block.** USA's 2024 → 2025 rate of -0.180/yr destroyed in ONE YEAR what other federal systems took 10-15 years to do.

Federal-friction breaks when:
1. **National supreme/constitutional court is captured** (USA SCOTUS 6-3 in 2020; MEX recent constitutional court reform; IDN Constitutional Court erosion)
2. **State-level party apparatus aligns with national** (USA GOP state-level machinery; MEX Morena state machines)
3. **Single-event triggers bypass federal procedures** (USA executive orders + civil-service purge; MEX constitutional reforms via Morena supermajority)

**Sub-pattern**: federal-friction slow-burn → court capture → state-machine alignment → rate breakout.

## Year-by-year USA breakout

USA's full-panel rate is -0.0089/yr but recent data shows acceleration:
- 1998-2013: 0.810 → 0.852 (mild improvement)
- 2013-2020: 0.852 → 0.734 (Trump I, -0.118 over 7y = -0.017/yr, federal-friction era)
- 2020-2024: 0.734 → 0.751 (Biden partial recovery)
- **2024-2025: 0.751 → 0.571 (-0.180 in 1 year, federal-friction broken)**

The breakout corresponds with:
- SCOTUS 6-3 stable since 2020
- GOP party purge of dissidents 2021-2024 (Liz Cheney etc)
- State-level election infrastructure capture in GOP states
- Project 2025 institutional preparation
- Federal civil service purge plans (Schedule F revival)
