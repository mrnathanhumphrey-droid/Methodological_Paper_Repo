## Resolution: did Castillo's failed coup ENABLE Boluarte's success?

**Reading A — Enabled (political-establishment realignment)**:
- Pre-Castillo: political establishment was fragmented across Castillo (left) vs Congress (right) vs judiciary (independent)
- Castillo's self-coup attempt (Dec 2022) ELIMINATED Castillo as a political force AND united the right-wing political establishment around Boluarte
- Boluarte's mass-protest crackdowns (Dec 2022-Jan 2023, 60+ deaths suppressing pro-Castillo protests) faced limited domestic resistance because political class saw them as preventing Castillo's return
- Boluarte then used this consolidated political position to capture high court (hcind collapsed 1.547 → 0.455) + civil society + opposition

**Reading B — Independent (Boluarte mechanism would happen anyway)**:
- PER had institutional fragility (5 presidents 2016-2022) before Castillo
- Boluarte's mechanism is REGULAR aggrandizement (legal/civilian), not coup-triggered
- Failed coup is coincident with Boluarte rise but not causally necessary

**Verdict**: Reading A is supported. Boluarte's faster backsliding rate ≥ pre-Castillo rate is hard to explain without the political-establishment realignment caused by Castillo's failed coup. The institutional resistance that would normally check executive aggrandizement was suppressed by the political class's commitment to preventing Castillo's return.

## Generalized sub-pattern: failed-coup-as-enabler

PER is the cleanest case of a broader sub-pattern. Other instances:

### TUR 2016 failed coup → Erdoğan consolidation acceleration
- Gulenist faction attempts coup July 15, 2016 (failed within 24 hours)
- Erdoğan uses failed coup as pretext for mass purges (150,000+ purged from civil service, military, academia, judiciary)
- 2017 constitutional referendum (presidential system) passes
- libdem trajectory accelerates: 2014 (0.252) → 2016 (0.143) [pre-coup] → 2017 (0.101) [post-coup]
- Failed coup CONFIRMED as accelerator

### VEN 2002 failed coup against Chávez → Chávez consolidation acceleration
- Brief 2-day coup against Chávez (April 11-13, 2002) fails
- Chávez uses event to justify packing Supreme Court (2004) + Bolivarian consolidation
- libdem trajectory: 2002 (0.255) → 2003 (0.243) → 2007 (0.162) — accelerated post-failed-coup
- Failed coup CONFIRMED as accelerator

### USA Jan 6, 2021 → Trump II preparation
- Failed insurrection at US Capitol Jan 6, 2021
- GOP responds by purging dissidents (Liz Cheney, Adam Kinzinger removed from leadership)
- Project 2025 institutional preparation begins
- 2024 Trump re-election + Trump II rapid consolidation
- Failed coup-adjacent event likely ENABLED Trump II preparation (4-year gap shows party-state-machinery built between failure and renewed attempt)

## Implication for PATTERN_027 (failed-backsliding archive)

**PATTERN_027's claim**: 5 cases of failed backsliding (BRA, ISR, USA Trump I, KOR, PER Castillo) with 4 blocking levers (court + civil society + electoral cycle + federal counter-pressure).

**P1-F refinement**: blocking a coup or self-coup attempt is NOT the same as preventing backsliding. The PER case shows: failed coup attempts can ENABLE subsequent regular-government backsliding via political-establishment realignment. Add to PATTERN_027:

- **Blocked successfully** (sustained democracy): KOR Dec 2024 Yoon (Yoon impeached, succession via legitimate process), BRA Bolsonaro (lost 2022 election, peaceful transition)
- **Blocked attempt but successor consolidates** (PER pattern): Castillo failed coup → Boluarte consolidation
- **Failed coup as accelerator** (TUR/VEN pattern): coup target uses event to accelerate own consolidation

So PATTERN_027's 5 "blocked" cases need re-examination:
- KOR: Yoon impeached + libdem recovery 2025 (+0.112) — TRUE block
- BRA: Bolsonaro lost election + Lula recovery — TRUE block (but failed Jan 8 attacks didn't enable Lula consolidation)
- USA Trump I: lost 2020 election but Trump II 2024 returns — DELAYED rather than blocked
- PER Castillo self-coup: blocked the coup, but Boluarte consolidation followed — PARTIAL block
- ISR judicial overhaul: blocked legislation, but war reshaped politics — partial / different mechanism

**Refined PATTERN_027 typology**:
- TRUE BLOCK: KOR, BRA (libdem recovery >0.10 sustained 2+ years)
- DELAYED: USA Trump I (failure→ 4y gap → return)
- DISPLACED: PER (failed coup attempt → different mechanism backslides)
- POSTPONED: ISR (legislative defeat but threat remains)

## Closure of P1-F

PER complication is RESOLVED: Castillo's failed self-coup did not directly cause Boluarte's backsliding, but it created the political-establishment realignment that ENABLED faster consolidation by the successor government. This is part of a broader sub-pattern (TUR 2016, VEN 2002) where failed coup attempts accelerate or displace backsliding rather than prevent it.

**Sub-pattern identified**: **failed-coup-as-enabler** — a forward-prediction-relevant mechanism for cases where failed self-coups or coups against autocrats produce political-establishment realignments that subsequently enable consolidation.
