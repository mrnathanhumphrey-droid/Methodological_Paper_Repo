## One line
USA libdem dropped **0.751 → 0.571 in a single year** (2024→2025), a Δ of **−0.180** — globally a **1-in-700 country-year event** (only 36 of 25,464 V-Dem observations 1789-2025 recorded a larger one-year drop). The decline maps PERFECTLY onto Sato et al. 2022's horizontal → diagonal → vertical sequence in a single year, with vertical accountability not yet touched because no federal election happened in 2025.

## Why it stands out

### Globally extreme magnitude
- 1-in-700 country-year event (Nord et al., V-Dem 2026, secondary citation)
- Lowest US libdem since 1965
- Global rank moved from 20th (2024) to 51st of 179 (2025)
- V-Dem Institute 2025 description: "the fastest evolving episode of autocratization the USA has been through"
- Potentially "the fastest autocratizing country in contemporary history that does not involve a coup d'état"

### Perfect Sato 2022 sequence in a SINGLE YEAR
The horizontal → diagonal → vertical order Sato established across 69 episodes (typically unfolding over years) happened in USA 2024→2025 in **one year**:
1. **Horizontal accountability**: −0.757 (executive oversight, legislative checks on executive, fired IGs, agency captures, DOJ purges)
2. **Diagonal accountability**: −0.654 (media censorship index dropped 1.61!, civil society space dropped 0.88, free expression dropped 0.18)
3. **Vertical accountability**: −0.003 (essentially unchanged — no federal election in 2025; the test is 2026 midterms)

The compressed timeline of Sato's framework playing out in real-time validates the framework AND establishes USA 2025 as the upper-bound speed case.

### Comparison to closest case
The closest in-corpus speed comparator is **SLV/Bukele** at −0.061/yr. USA 2024→2025 = **−0.180/yr** — almost **3× faster than the previously-fastest SLV signature**.

### Distinct from SLV-style consolidation
Carnegie 2025 (Carrier & Carothers, August): Trump II is NOT the incremental Orban/PiS pattern. The closest comparator is SLV/Bukele's fast-pole *style* but USA exceeds even SLV's velocity by 3×. Mechanism: single dramatic event style + a federal executive branch with unprecedented unilateral capacities + a captured Supreme Court (6-3) + a captured GOP party machine + no EU-style external constraints.

## Implications for PATTERN_013 and the substrate framework

1. **USA is the empirical proof-of-concept of Sato 2022's framework in real-time** — replicates horizontal-first chronology cleanly in single-year data.
2. **Fast-pole within the third-wave class**: extends our speed-variation finding from −0.014 (IND) to −0.180 (USA), a 13× range across cases.
3. **2025 ≠ 2028**: vertical accountability hasn't dropped because no election. The 2026 midterms will be the critical vertical-accountability test.
4. **Federal-friction slow-burn doesn't apply** — USA is federal but Trump II is bypassing federal friction via direct executive action + federal court capture + congressional party-state fusion (GOP).
