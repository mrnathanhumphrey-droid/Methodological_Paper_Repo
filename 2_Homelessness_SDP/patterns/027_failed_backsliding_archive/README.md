## One line
Not all backsliding attempts succeed. Across our enlarged corpus, **5 cases show executive-aggrandizement attempts that were BLOCKED before consolidation completed**: BRA (Bolsonaro 2019-2022), ISR (Netanyahu/Levin judicial overhaul 2023), USA (Trump I 2017-2020), KOR (Yoon martial law Dec 2024), PER (Castillo self-coup attempt 2022). Each gives a different blocking mechanism — together they identify the structural levers that resist Bukelization.

## What separates blocked from successful backsliding

Comparing 5 blocked cases vs 7 successful Bukelization cases:

| Factor | Blocked (BRA, ISR, USA-I, KOR, PER-Castillo) | Successful (SLV, HUN, TUR, VEN, POL, TUN, BLR) |
|---|---|---|
| Independent Supreme/Constitutional Court | YES (BRA STF, USA SCOTUS, KOR Constitutional Court) | NO (captured early — HUN 2/3 majority, POL refusal-to-seat, etc.) |
| Mass civil society mobilization | YES (ISR protests, KOR protests, BRA Jan 8 backlash) | LIMITED (HUN protest movements failed to coordinate; TUR 2013 Gezi suppressed) |
| Electoral defeat of incumbent | YES (USA 2020, BRA 2022) | NO (incumbents won repeated elections) |
| Federal/regional counter-pressure | YES (BRA states, USA states) | NO (most cases are unitary or weakly federal) |
| Military refusal | RELEVANT (KOR Dec 2024) | N/A (none of the successful cases involved direct military mobilization) |
| Single-event vs incremental attempt | SINGLE-EVENT (KOR martial law, PER Castillo self-coup, ISR overhaul, USA Trump I impulses) | INCREMENTAL (HUN, POL, TUR step-by-step) |

**Strong pattern**: Single-dramatic-event attempts FAIL more often than incremental legalistic consolidations succeed. The exception is SLV (single-event-via-state-of-exception that DID succeed — because Bukele had 80%+ approval + Constituent Assembly that he'd already captured).

## The 4 mechanism levers that block backsliding

From the 5 cases:
1. **Independent Constitutional/Supreme Court**: BRA STF, USA SCOTUS, KOR Constitutional Court, ISR Supreme Court. Where this is captured early (HUN, POL, VEN), backsliding succeeds.
2. **Mass civil society capable of sustained mobilization**: ISR protests, KOR protests, BRA Jan 8 unifying backlash. Required coordinated party-and-civil-society infrastructure.
3. **Electoral cycle within reach**: USA 2020, BRA 2022. If election happens during backsliding attempt, it's the natural reset mechanism.
4. **Federal/regional governance with parallel power**: USA states, BRA states. Distributes points of resistance.

When 3+ of these are present, backsliding fails. When 2 or fewer, it succeeds.

- State-level resistance + federal civil service → lever #4 partially intact

Hence USA 2025 = fast-pole instead of plateau.
