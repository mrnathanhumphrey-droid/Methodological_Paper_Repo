## 1. Hypothesis

**H1 (federal-friction-as-temporary-block):** Federal-system countries undergoing executive aggrandizement decline at a rate 2-5× slower than non-federal cases (-0.009 to -0.017 libdem/yr vs -0.033 to -0.061 for non-federal). This federal-friction is real but TEMPORARY.

**H2 (breakability conditions):** Federal-friction BREAKS — rate accelerates to non-federal Bukelization speeds — when ALL THREE hold:
- (a) National supreme/constitutional court is captured (justices appointed by current regime > 50% of bench)
- (b) State-level party apparatus aligns with national governing party in majority of states
- (c) Single-event trigger (executive order, constitutional reform, or supermajority legislation) bypasses federal procedures

**H3 (USA 2024-2025 is the textbook breakout):** USA federal-friction slow-burn era 1998-2023 (rate -0.0089/yr) broke in 2024-2025 (rate -0.180/yr) after all 3 breakability conditions held.
