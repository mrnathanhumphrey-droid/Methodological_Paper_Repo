## 1. Hypothesis

**H1 (mirror-order recovery):** In any executive-aggrandizement backsliding episode that subsequently undergoes institutional restoration, the order of sub-indicator RECOVERY is the REVERSE of the order of sub-indicator CAPTURE.

**Sato 2022 capture order**: horizontal → diagonal → vertical (across 69 episodes)
**Predicted recovery order**: vertical-LAST, horizontal-FIRST → recovery moves UPSTREAM through the capture sequence

**H2 (mechanism):** Institutional captures stack DOWNSTREAM during consolidation (court capture enables election-law changes enables media regulation enables civil-society restrictions). Recovery operates by UNCAPTURING the most-recent / most-superficial captures first, while deeper / earlier captures persist longer.

**H3 (specific prediction):** Elections-free-and-fair (v2elfrfair) and opposition autonomy (v2psoppaut) — the vertical-accountability sub-indicators captured LAST chronologically — are the LAST to recover.

## 4. Falsifiers

- **F1:** Expanded recovery case test shows vertical-tier ABOVE horizontal-tier in ≥3 of 8 new cases → mirror-order pattern is artifact of original 5 cases
- **F2:** In real-time tracking, a country shows vertical recovery BEFORE horizontal recovery → reverse ordering possible
- **F3:** A forward-watch recovery shows uniform recovery across sub-indicators → no sequence effect; institutional bundle moves together
- **F4:** Historical transition cases (CHL, IDN, KOR-1987) show different pattern entirely (because baseline-was-already-low artifact) → mirror-order only applies to recoveries from active backsliding, not from prior authoritarianism

F1 OR F2 (in ≥2 forward cases) firing = mirror-order walked back.
